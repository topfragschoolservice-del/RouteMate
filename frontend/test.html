📖 Quick Start Instructions

  1. Parent Dashboard: Click "Open Parent Dashboard", select a parent from dropdown, enter email, click Login
  2. Driver Dashboard: Click "Open Driver Dashboard", select a driver from dropdown, enter email, click Login
  3. Tracking Page: On Driver Dashboard, click "Start Tracking", then view live updates on Tracking page
  4. Mark Attendance: On Parent Dashboard, select student and check morning/afternoon boxes
  5. Make Payment: On Parent Dashboard, enter amount and click "Pay"
  6. Update Location: On Driver Dashboard, enter latitude/longitude and click "Update Location"
